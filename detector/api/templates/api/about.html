About Us

We are a team of high school students committed to improving the online experience. After seeing how certain websites use misleading tactics, known as dark patterns, to influence users' decisions, we developed a browser extension to detect and highlight these practices. Our goal is to help users browse the web more confidently by providing them with clear information about when they might be manipulated. We believe in fostering a more transparent and user-friendly internet for everyone.
